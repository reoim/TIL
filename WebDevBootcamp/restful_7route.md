# 7 Restful Routes

| **URL** | **HTTP Verb** |  **Action**|
|------------|-------------|------------|
| /dogs/         | GET       | index  
| /dogs/new      | GET       | new   
| /dogs          | POST      | create   
| /dogs/:id      | GET       | show       
| /dogs/:id/edit | GET       | edit       
| /dogs/:id      | PATCH/PUT | update    
| /dogs/:id      | DELETE    | destroy
